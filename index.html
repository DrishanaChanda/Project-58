My favourite cartoon

Shinchan

I like to watch Shinchan

Shin-chan,is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshito Usui. The animated television adaptation began airing on TV Asahi in 1992 and is still ongoing on several television networks worldwide. It has over 1000 episodes and 26 full-length movies. The show has been dubbed in 30 languages which aired in 45 countries.
